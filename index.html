About Me

I create growth opportunities—and make them real.

My work sits at the intersection of strategy, org design, and operating economics—clarifying where to play, how to win, and how to stand up the teams, processes, and dashboards that keep everyone rowing in the same direction.

At iBrain (The International Institute for the Brain), we tripled in size during my tenure and launched additional business lines. I helped shape the organization’s lateral expansion into healthcare and education, supporting leadership with the financial and operational models to scale responsibly—flying the plane while we build it.

What I do

  • Growth design & economics: market sizing, pricing, unit economics, and portfolio bets—so growth pays for itself.
  • Org & process restructuring: right roles, spans, workflows, and governance to move faster with clarity.
  • Execution systems: roadmaps, cadence, and transparent reporting so plans survive first contact with reality.
  • Modeling that drives action: revenue engines (rates, payer mix, volumes), cost architecture (people, space, vendors), capacity & staffing models, cash‑flow and working‑capital planning, and scenario/sensitivity analysis to de‑risk decisions.
